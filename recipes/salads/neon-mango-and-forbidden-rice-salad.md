# Neon Mango & Forbidden Rice Salad

> *Jet-black forbidden rice meets vivid mango, crisp cucumber, and a zippy lime-sesame dressing in this AI-conceived salad that's as striking to look at as it is to eat.*

**Prep time:** 20 minutes  
**Cook time:** 30 minutes (rice)  
**Servings:** 4  
**Difficulty:** Easy

---

## Ingredients

**Salad**
- 1 cup black (forbidden) rice, cooked and cooled
- 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and diced
- 1 English cucumber, diced
- 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
- ½ cup edamame, shelled and cooked
- ¼ cup fresh cilantro leaves
- 3 green onions, thinly sliced
- 2 tablespoons toasted sesame seeds

**Lime-Sesame Dressing**
- 3 tablespoons fresh lime juice
- 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
- 1 tablespoon toasted sesame oil
- 1 tablespoon honey (or maple syrup for vegan)
- 1 teaspoon soy sauce (or tamari for gluten-free)
- 1 teaspoon freshly grated ginger
- 1 small garlic clove, minced

## Instructions

1. Cook black rice according to package directions. Spread on a baking sheet and let cool to room temperature.
2. Whisk all dressing ingredients together in a small bowl. Taste and adjust lime or honey as needed.
3. Combine cooled rice, mango, cucumber, bell pepper, and edamame in a large bowl.
4. Pour dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat.
5. Top with cilantro, green onions, and sesame seeds. Serve immediately or refrigerate up to 4 hours.

## Notes

- Brown rice or quinoa can substitute for forbidden rice.
- Add sliced avocado just before serving for extra creaminess.
- For a protein boost, toss in grilled shrimp or cubed tofu.
